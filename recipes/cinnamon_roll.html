Delectable Cinnamon Rolls

This is a classic pastry that everyone knows and loves. The smell of the warm sticky sweet cinnamon filling is enough to drive anyone crazy.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 3/4 cup milk
    • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 egg & 1 egg yoke
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
    • 3 cups bread flour
    • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Filling
    • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
    • 1 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • Frosting
    • 4 oz cream cheese
    • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
    • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

The list of ingredients can seem intimidating at first, but I promise that the rewards are plenty! Let's start whipping these bad boys up.

Directions

  1. Add yeast to warm milk. Add the sugar, eggs, butter and stir. After the yeast has activated, add in the salt and flour. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  2. Place dough in a bowl and cover with a towel. Let rise for 1 - 1 1/2 hours
  3. After the dough has risen, roll out the dough into a 14x9 inch rectangle. Spread the butter on the dough rectangle and coat with the cinnamon and brown sugar.
  4. Roll the dough up and cut the roll into 1 inch sections
  5. place the rolls into a 9x9 inch baking pan. Let rise for another 30 minutes
  6. Place the pan in a 350 degree oven for 20-25 minutes
  7. Mix the cream cheese, butter, vanilla, and powdered sugar in a mixer for a couple of minutes. wait ten minutes for the rolls to cool down before applying the icing.Enjoy!
