Cinnamon Rolls

This is a classic pastry that everyone knows and loves. The smell of the warm sticky sweet cinnamon filling is enough to drive anyone crazy.

Ingredients

  • dough
    • 3/4 cup milk
    • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 egg & 1 egg yoke
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
    • 3 cups bread flour
    • 3/4 tsp salt
  • filling
    • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
    • 1 1/2 tbsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • frosting
    • 4 oz cream cheese
    • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
    • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
    • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
