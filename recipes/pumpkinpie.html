Pumpkin Pie

Who hasn't woken up in the middle of autumn thinking about the delicate texture and beautifully spiced flavor of pumpkin pie? I know I certainly have! Many families have recipes that have been handed down generation after generation. There is no better feeling than eating a delicious dessert knowing that you are eating a piece of tradition. This particular recipe was handed down to me via the internet today. Enjoy!

Ingredients

There are only 8 ingredients that we will be working with today

  • 15 oz canned pumpkin
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 9 inch unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Mix pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt until the mixture reaches a smooth consistency. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce the oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue to bake for 35-40 minutes.
  3. This is personally my favorite step. Garnish however your heart desires and enjoy!
